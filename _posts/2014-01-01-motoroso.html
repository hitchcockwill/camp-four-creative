Sorting & Filtering

Any car lover knows that there are infinitely many ways to categorize cars, especially when you start including aftermarket parts and customizations. We worked with the Motoroso team to figure out exactly how people generally categorize vehicles in their minds, then created this macro + micro filtering system to help users find exactly what they’re looking for quickly and easily.

One of Motoroso's priorities was to present vast amounts of information—part numbers, videos, polls, and more—while keeping things simple and intuitive. This photo page was designed to let the main photo shine, while still making it easy to access additional information.
