Spinnakr.com Footprint

Spinnakr is a SAAS product that gives businesses of all sizes access to insight driven analytics that takes action for them in real time.

We worked closely with Spinnakr to revamp their marketing pages with the goal of increasing requests to see the demo and attracting more job applicants.
