Our Philosophy

We believe that good design works hard.

Whether it’s making a sale, getting an email address, or just keeping a user engaged longer, every design we create has a clearly defined objective. We set these goals at the beginning of a project and meet them by gathering constant feedback, measuring results, and iterating often.

Who we are

As previous founders and employees of Silicon Valley startups, we understand what it takes to start a business and bring a product to market. We founded Camp Four Creative to fuel our passion for working with startups and small businesses.

We believe that our best work is produced by living a life full of “outside the office” inspiration, which is why you’ll often find us out hiking, backpacking, climbing, kayaking, biking, skiing, and enjoying many other outdoor activities in our spare time.

Alyssa Pelletier is a product designer who focuses on creating web and mobile (and sometimes print!) experiences that are simple, intuitive, and visually pleasing.

Will Hitchcock is a front end developer with a background in graphic design. He is passionate about web app development, data visualization, & writing clean, elegant code.
